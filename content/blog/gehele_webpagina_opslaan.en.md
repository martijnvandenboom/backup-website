In the web browser Microsoft Edge:
> click the 3 dots in the top right corner: ...
> choose the option: Web capture (Ctrl+Shift+S)
> choose the option: Capture full page
> click the button: Save
> an image of type .jpeg will now be saved in your personal directory: Downloads
