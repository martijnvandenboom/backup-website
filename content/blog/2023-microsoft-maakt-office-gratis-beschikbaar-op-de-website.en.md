Do you already have a Microsoft account?

Step 1.: log in to the website: office.com

Step 2.: click on the icon with the 9 dots in the top left of the screen

Step 3.: choose the Office app you want to use</br></br>



If you don't have a Microsoft account yet you can create one for free.

Step 1.: go to the website: account.microsoft.com

Step 2.: click the sign in button

Step 3.: click the link: Create an account now

Step 4.: fill in the requested information, store your password in a safe place
