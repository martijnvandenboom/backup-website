Change your Windows password by using a shortcut on your desktop. (CTRL+ALT+DEL key combination by clicking a shortcut)

1. Right-click on your desktop, choose New and then Shortcut

2. Enter the following command for the item location:\
C:\Windows\explorer.exe shell:::{2559a1f2-21d7-11d4-bdaf-00c04f60b9f0}

3. Click: Next

4. Give the shortcut a name, for example: Reset password

5. Click: Finish
