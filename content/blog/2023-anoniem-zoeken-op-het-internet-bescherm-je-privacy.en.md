Do you want to stay anonymous when searching the Internet?

Then use a different web browser. The Tor Browser was specifically developed to stay anonymous.</br></br>

Step 1.: go to the website: https://www.torproject.org/

Step 2.: download the Tor Browser

Step 3.: install the new application</br></br>

Please note! Do not change the size of the application window, as this can already reveal information about your identity. The advice: do not do this.
